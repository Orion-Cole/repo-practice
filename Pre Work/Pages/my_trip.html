My Alaskan Trip

Last winter I had the chance to visit Alaska for a week and it was an amazing experience. I stayed at Chena Hotsprings an hour north of Fairbanks and had the opportunity to ride in a dog sled, drive a Ski-doo, swim in the hot springs and even got to see moose!
It was a beautiful yet inhospitable place where I met amazing people, ate delicious food and nearly froze to death on top of a mountain. 😅
